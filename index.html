DevOps Camp 2015

A Camp for the DevOps Community

July 16th & 17th · New York, NY

A two day long convocation of some of the best engineers on the web at The United Nations for an in-depth exploration of all things DevOps, including a Configuration Management Roundtable. The DevOps camp will focus on everything Dev, Ops, NoOps, continuous delivery/integration, and everything in between - delving into all of the latest technology and trends, with real, tailored and informative content. In addition to hosting really great sessions, the DevOps camp will have a number of breakout groups as well as hands-on sessions, which will all be topped off with some great networking opportunities.
